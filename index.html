01. PC Zoom Keys

Use '+' to zoom in and '-' to zoom out on your keyboard for instant scaling.

02. PC Toolbox Key

Press 'Left Arrow' to slide the toolbox open and 'Right Arrow' to hide it.

03. Mobile Swiping

Swipe left from the right edge to open tools. Swipe right anywhere to close them.

04. Two Finger Pinch

On mobile or touch screens, use two fingers to pinch and scale the clock size.
